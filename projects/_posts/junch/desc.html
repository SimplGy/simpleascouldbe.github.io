Start by clicking on the game, so it knows to pay attention to your keystrokes.

W, S, A, and D move your character.

Spacebar jumps.

Clicking the mouse does a super butter punch.

This was a a solution to an AI/pathfinding and basic collision detection problem. Notice that the yenFlies™ will pursue the toast marble but will avoid colliding with each other.

Written in ActionScript 2.0
